Using the Traveline app to plan your walk

Jan 14, 2016

The Traveline app is key ingredient to planning a drive-free walk.

Journey Planner

The journey planner naturally lets you type the start and end points for your journey. You only need a few characters before it starts to bring back the bus stops around the area.

Favourite Bus Stops

This is a great feature to help you locate bus times at your regular stops. Just select a stop and it will show you all the buses which are due to stop there in the next hour.

Bus Stop Finder

Have you ever not been able to find a bus stop. Sometimes they are not at the exact location on the route map or the stop on the return route may not be immediately opposite.
