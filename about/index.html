About Drive Free Walks

My inspiration for creating Drive Free Walks originates from my combined love of both walking and public transport.

Driving to walks can become tricky when you have to navigate down country lanes which you are unfamiliar with. Then after the walk, when all you want to do is rest your limbs, you need to force yourself to concentrate on the drive home.

Thatâ€™s when I came up with the idea for planning my walking routes to start and stop at train stations or within reach of the bus network. It is great being able to explore train lines and bus routes which I would otherwise have no reason to visit. Once I had done a few drive-free walks, I decided it was time to share my experiences on a blog.

I hope you enjoy reading through my blog and feel inspired to try drive-free walking!
