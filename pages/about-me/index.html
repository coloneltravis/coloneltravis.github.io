Two of my favourite activities from childhood were writing stories playing around with my ZX Spectrum. I chose computers as my career path which has provided me with 20 years working as a software developer.

I have a wealth of IT experience across a range of industries including news, research, logistics, telecommunications, broadcasting, education and health.

For the last 10 years I have worked with web technologies which has provided me with in-depth knowledge of the inner structure of the Internet. I have now reached a stage where I would like to rediscover my other childhood activity of writing. What better place for me to start than to write about technology! I love researching and learning new technologies and then translating them to a less technical language.

I also intend to use this blog to practice and show some of my fictional writing. Please feel free to read them and leave a comment. I will value your feedback and use it to improve my writing.

Writing provides an opportunity to diverse into many other areas, so expect to see some articles which may appear to be off-topic, like travel or walking.

My writing journey starts here. What path will it take me on?
